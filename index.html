About Me

I am a fourth-year CS Ph.D. candidate at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS). Prior to NUS, I received my B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from National Chiao Tung University (Taiwan). My research focuses on machine learning techniques applied to computer vision tasks, especially, visual scene understanding and reasoning. I use Python and PyTorch.
